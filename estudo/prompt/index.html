PROMPT 1 — RAPID MARKET SNAPSHOT Qualquer AI
RAPID MARKET SNAPSHOT
Cria a fundação: quem é o avatar, qual o posicionamento, quais problemas resolve. Briefing estratégico que alimenta todo o resto.
#Role You are a strategic marketing analyst trained in rapid onboarding and positioning discovery for direct response health offers in the American 50+ market. #Goal Generate a short, high-clarity writeup answering: Who are we targeting, what are we selling, and what problems are we solving? #Context My ideal customer persona is: [INSERIR AQUI] The product I want to sell is: [INSERIR AQUI] The problems my product is solving: [INSERIR AQUI] #Task Write a few short paragraphs that clearly answer the three context questions. Include: - A one-paragraph sketch of the customer persona (demographics, psychographics, motivations) - A 1–2 sentence description of the product and positioning - A short explanation of the core problems this product resolves Keep language simple, human, no marketing fluff. #Output Plain paragraphs, not lists. Write as if handing off to a creative strategist who needs to understand the market at a glance before diving into Deep Research.
PROMPT 2 — QUOTES REAIS DO REDDIT (DEEP RESEARCH) Deep Research / Perplexity Pro
QUOTES REAIS DO REDDIT (DEEP RESEARCH)
O coração do Elemento X. Joga a AI dentro do Reddit e extrai as palavras reais que a audiência usa pra descrever sua dor. Quotes verbatim, sem filtro.
#Role You are a world-class market research analyst with 10+ years specializing in extracting exact customer language from Reddit for direct response health advertising. #Goal Gather 100 verbatim, word-for-word quotes from Reddit that my target customers use when discussing their fears, frustrations, wants, beliefs, and joys. Produce a detailed persona sketch informed by these authentic voices. #Context My ideal customer persona is: [INSERIR AQUI] The product I want to sell is: [INSERIR AQUI] The problems my product is solving: [INSERIR AQUI] I want to deeply understand the motivations, pain points, and aspirations that drive my ideal customer — using their own words and tone. #Task 1. Identify and prioritize relevant subreddits. 2. Search threads and comments from the past 6 months, ensuring current language and sentiment. 3. Extract 20 raw, word-for-word quotes per category (total: 100), preserving slang, typos, and emotion: - My prospect fears are… - My prospect feels frustrated by… - My prospect wants… - My prospect believes… - My prospect enjoys… 4. Label each quote with the subreddit/thread source. 5. Synthesize a 5–6 sentence persona sketch capturing the emotional landscape and driving motivations. #Output Clear, organized, numbered list by category. Each quote in quotation marks with subreddit label. Persona sketch at the end in plain language.
PROMPT 3 — PSYCHOGRAPHIC RESEARCH Qualquer AI
PSYCHOGRAPHIC RESEARCH
Organiza as quotes em 4 buckets psicográficos (Identidade, Problemas, Desejos, Obstáculos). Os padrões que emergem revelam sub-personas.
#Goal Produce an in-depth psychographic profile using Eugene Schwartz's research methods. Map Identity, Problems, Dreams/Desires, and Obstacles so our copy sounds like it was written from inside the community. #Context My ideal customer persona is: [INSERIR AQUI] The product I want to sell is: [INSERIR AQUI] The problems my product is solving: [INSERIR AQUI] #Task 1. For each of the 4 categories below, gather at least 5 distinct themes. Under every theme, collect 2–4 word-for-word quotes (include slang, typos, emotion): - Identity (who they believe they are) - Problems (what keeps them up at night) - Dreams / Desires (what they truly want) - Obstacles (why they haven't solved it yet) 2. Label every quote with the source subreddit/thread. 3. Beneath each theme write a short Note that sums up the pattern. 4. Synthesize a 5–6 sentence persona sketch. #Output Clean, ordered document by category → theme → quotes → pattern note. Persona sketch at the end.
PROMPT 4 — AWARENESS + WILL / WON'T / CAN'T TELL Qualquer AI
AWARENESS + WILL / WON'T / CAN'T TELL
Expande cada desire com a escada "So I can..." e mapeia Will Tell / Won't Tell / Can't Tell. A camada emocional mais profunda.
#Goal Surface the deepest market desires and awareness-level questions of our target audience using Eugene Schwartz's methodology. #Context My ideal customer persona is: [INSERIR AQUI] The product I want to sell is: [INSERIR AQUI] The problems my product is solving: [INSERIR AQUI] #Task 1. Market Awareness Levels Table Build a table with 5 columns: Most Aware, Product Aware, Solution Aware, Problem Aware, Unaware. For each column list the key questions a prospect at that stage is likely asking. 2. Market Desires Expansion Identify 5–10 explicit desires. Create a "So I can…" ladder for each (3 levels deep). Table: Desire | So I can… (L1) | So I can… (L2) | So I can… (L3) 3. Will / Won't / Can't Tell Table For every desire above: - Will Tell — what they say openly - Won't Tell — what feels too private or embarrassing - Can't Tell — hidden or subconscious drivers Table: Desire | Will Tell | Won't Tell | Can't Tell #Output 3 tables in order, formatted cleanly for copy/paste.
Timing — O que está acontecendo agora
PROMPT 5 — TREND SCANNER (DEEP RESEARCH) Deep Research / Perplexity Pro
TREND SCANNER (DEEP RESEARCH)
Varre o que está acontecendo AGORA — esta semana, este mês. Conversas emergentes, ingredientes trending, medos novos, formatos virais, mudanças regulatórias.
#Role You are a real-time market intelligence analyst specialized in identifying emerging trends, breaking conversations, and sentiment shifts in the health and wellness market. You monitor Reddit, TikTok, YouTube, Google Trends, PubMed, FDA announcements, and health news outlets. #Goal Produce a comprehensive trend report for the [NICHE] market, capturing what is happening RIGHT NOW — this week, this month — that creates new emotional triggers, new angles, new fears, or new opportunities that marketers can leverage immediately. #Context Niche: [INSERIR NICHO — ex: Erectile Dysfunction supplements for men 50+] Target audience: [INSERIR AUDIÊNCIA — ex: American men 50-70 with ED] Current product type: [INSERIR TIPO — ex: Natural supplement / Telehealth prescription / Compounded formula] #Task Search across Reddit, TikTok, YouTube, Google Trends, PubMed, FDA.gov, and major health news sites for the MOST RECENT developments (last 30 days, prioritize last 7 days). Organize findings into 5 categories: 1. EMERGING CONVERSATIONS New discussion threads gaining traction on Reddit and social media. Topics that are being talked about NOW that weren't being talked about 60 days ago. Include exact quotes and thread links where possible. 2. TRENDING INGREDIENTS / TREATMENTS New ingredients, compounds, formulations, or treatment approaches gaining attention — either positive or negative. Include source of attention (TikTok trend, new study, influencer mention, Reddit thread). 3. NEW FEARS & CONCERNS Negative news, side effect reports, FDA warnings, lawsuits, or alarming studies that are creating NEW fear in the market right now. These are urgent emotional triggers that didn't exist recently. 4. VIRAL CONTENT FORMATS What types of organic content about this niche are getting high engagement right now on TikTok, YouTube, and Reddit? Describe the format, tone, and why it's working. These represent the audience's current "native content consumption" pattern. 5. REGULATORY & NEWS SHIFTS FDA announcements, legal changes, insurance coverage updates, pricing shifts, supply chain issues, or mainstream media coverage that is actively changing the market landscape. For each finding, include: - What it is (specific, not vague) - Where it's happening (platform, subreddit, source) - When it emerged (date or timeframe) - Why it matters for advertising (emotional trigger, angle opportunity, or market gap) #Output Organized by the 5 categories above. Each finding as a short paragraph with source. Prioritize findings with the highest ADVERTISING POTENTIAL — meaning those that can be turned into ad angles, hooks, or VSL narratives immediately.
PROMPT 6 — CRUZAMENTO: PERSONA × TREND (GERADOR DE ÂNGULOS) Qualquer AI
CRUZAMENTO: PERSONA × TREND (GERADOR DE ÂNGULOS)
Cruza sub-personas (Profundidade) × trends (Timing). Resultado: matriz de ângulos com zona de escala e janela de tempo. O gerador de Zona 4.
#Role You are a creative strategist specialized in direct response advertising who identifies high-converting ad angles by systematically crossing audience psychology with real-time market trends. #Goal Generate a matrix of high-potential ad angles by crossing each persona discovered in the Deep Research (Phase 1) with each relevant trend from the Trend Scanner (Prompt 5). Each angle must be specific, actionable, and time-sensitive. #Context [PASTE THE OUTPUT OF YOUR PERSONA RESEARCH — Prompts 1-4 results, including personas, sub-personas, Ethos, Pathos levels, Will/Won't/Can't Tell] [PASTE THE OUTPUT OF PROMPT 5 — Trend Scanner results, all 5 categories] #Task For each persona and sub-persona identified: 1. ETHOS × TREND MATCH Which current trends align with this persona's source of trust? For example: if the persona trusts pastors and a trending TikTok format features faith-based health testimonials, that's a match. 2. PATHOS × TREND MATCH Which current trends activate this persona's deepest emotional layer (Pathos Level 3-4 / Can't Tell)? For example: if the persona's Can't Tell is "maybe God is punishing me" and a new study links a biblical ingredient to health benefits, that trend activates the deepest emotional layer. 3. FORMAT × TREND MATCH Which viral content formats from the trend scan match this persona's organic content consumption? This determines the ad container. 4. ANGLE SYNTHESIS For each viable cross, generate a complete angle brief: - Persona (specific name from research) - Trend (specific finding from scan) - Hook (first line of the ad, in the persona's language) - Emotional mechanism (which Pathos level it hits) - Format recommendation (based on viral format match) - Time window estimate (24h / 7 days / 30 days before saturation) - Scale zone (Zone 1-4 from the Depth × Timing framework) 5. PRIORITY RANKING Rank all angles by: - Zone 4 angles first (highest depth × timing) - Zone 3 next - Zone 2 as evergreen backlog #Output A numbered list of angle briefs, ranked by scale potential. Each brief should be complete enough for a copywriter to start writing immediately. Include at minimum 10 angles across the personas.
